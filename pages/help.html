Frequently Asked Questions
"What information do you collect?"
No personal data is collected on you besides your Username, Password, and Usage Statistics. You can read more here.
"I keep getting an Invalid Session error"
This error occurs when your Session ID is not the same when the page is loading as it was when you were directed there. You can try and disable Secure Session in the Settings to fix this.
"I forgot my password"
Currently, there is no way to regain access to an account without its password. We will be unable to help restore the account in most situations.
